How to recycle cardbaord.

  • Steps ↓
  • What happens next ↓

  1. Step 1

    Empty the box.
    Ensure it is mostly clean and dry.

  2. Notice

    A little grease is acceptable.

  3. Notice

    Labels and stickers do not have to be removed.

  4. Step 2

    Flatten the cardbaord box.

  5. Done!

    Place carton into recycling bin.

  6. Caution

    Do not place recycling in plastic bags.
