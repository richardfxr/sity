How to recycle cartons.

  • Steps ↓
  • What happens next ↓

  1. Step 1

    Empty the carton.
    Rinse if possible.

  2. Step 2

    Attach the plastic cap to the carton.

  3. Done!

    Place the carton into a recycling bin.

  4. Caution

    Do not place recycling in plastic bags.
